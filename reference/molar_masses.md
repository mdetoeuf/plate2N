# Record of molar masses for studied compounds

Originally, it contains molar masses of N, NO3-, NO2-, NH4+, but it can
easily be appended with additional molar masses for other relevant
applications

## Usage

``` r
molar_masses
```

## Format

A vector of named numerics containing relevant molar masses.

- N:

  molar mass of elemental nitrogen in g/mol (not N2)

- NO3:

  molar mass of nitrate in g/mol

- NO2:

  molar mass of nitrite in g/mol

- NH4:

  molar mass of ammonium in g/mol
